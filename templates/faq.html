FAQ

1.How does MealsonWings work?▼
We utilize cutting-edge drone technology to deliver your favorite meals from local restaurants directly to your doorstep. Simply place your order through our website or mobile app, and our drones will swiftly transport your meal to you, ensuring a fast and convenient dining experience.
2.What areas do you serve?▼
We currently operate in Hawthorn area. However, we're constantly expanding our service area, so be sure to check our website or app for updates on new locations.
3.How long does delivery take?▼
Delivery times may vary depending on factors such as order volume and weather conditions. However, our goal is to deliver your meal to you as quickly as possible. You can track the status of your order in real-time through our website or app.
4.What safety measures do you have in place?▼
Safety is our top priority. Our drones are equipped with advanced navigation systems and undergo regular maintenance to ensure safe and reliable operation. Additionally, we adhere to all regulations and guidelines set forth by aviation authorities to ensure the safety of our customers and the public.
5. Can I order from multiple restaurants in the same order?▼
Currently, we only support orders from a single restaurant per transaction. However, you can place multiple orders if you wish to order from different restaurants.
